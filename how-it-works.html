Vetting Process

How We Select AI Projects

Every project that launches on AIAccess must pass our rigorous 6-point vetting process. No exceptions.

👤

Public Team

No anonymous founders. Every core team member must be publicly identified and verifiable. Accountability is non-negotiable.

⚙️

Working Product

Projects must have a working demo or live product. We don't list vaporware — only real, usable technology gets featured.

🔐

Smart Contract Audit

All smart contracts must be audited by CertiK, Hacken, or SlowMist. Your investment security comes first.

📊

Fair Tokenomics

12+ month vesting for team tokens, no excessive insider allocations. Sustainable tokenomics that protect holders.

🧠

Unique Tech or Data

Projects must have a defensible technological edge — unique data, novel AI architecture, or proprietary systems that others can't easily replicate.

🌐

Real Community Traction

Genuine organic community engagement, not inflated bot numbers. We verify real user interest and social proof.
